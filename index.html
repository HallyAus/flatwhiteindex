How we built this

The method

Every price in the Flat White Index is verified by direct phone contact with the café. We don't scrape menus or rely on user reports. We call and ask: "How much is a small flat white?"

The data

847 cafés called across 23 Sydney suburbs. Prices collected between January and March 2026. Updated monthly. Sample sizes shown for every suburb — treat anything under 10 cafés with caution.

What's next

Phase 2 covers the Eastern Suburbs, North Shore, and Inner West expansion. Our goal: every independent café in Greater Sydney, priced and mapped.
