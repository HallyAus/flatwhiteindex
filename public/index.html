How it works

Four steps to every price

1.
Discover. We find every cafe in Sydney using Google Places — suburb by suburb, across the whole metro area.
2.
Call. Our AI voice agent Mia phones each cafe and asks one question: "How much is a regular flat white?"
3.
Extract. Prices are pulled from the call transcript, verified, and added to the index in real time.
4.
Map. Every price lands on this dashboard — sorted by suburb, plotted on the map, updated live.
