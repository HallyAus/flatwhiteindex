How we built this

The calls

An AI voice agent phones each café directly and asks one question: "How much is a regular flat white?" It handles voicemail, cafés that don't serve them, and the occasional hang-up. Each call averages 45 seconds.

The process

Cafés are discovered automatically from public business listings. Calls go out in small batches during business hours, weekdays only. Prices are extracted from the call transcripts and verified before publishing.

The data

847 cafés called across 23 Sydney suburbs so far. Chains excluded — only independent cafés. Prices collected January–March 2026, updated monthly. Sample sizes shown per suburb. Anything under 10 cafés, treat with caution.

What's next

Phase 2 expands to Greater Sydney — Eastern Suburbs, North Shore, Northern Beaches. Phase 3 hits Melbourne. The goal: every independent café in Australia's major cities, priced and mapped. Got a suburb you want us to call? Request it here.
