Methodology

How we collect this data

01

Mia calls every café

Our AI voice agent phones each café during business hours and asks a simple question: "How much is a regular flat white?" Real conversations, real prices.

02

Prices are verified

Every response is transcribed and parsed. We extract prices from natural speech — "$4.50", "four fifty", "about five bucks" — and flag ambiguous answers for human review.

03

Data goes live

Verified prices are published instantly. Every data point links to a real café and a real phone call. No scraping, no guessing. Chains excluded — independents only.
