// Methodology

How It Works

01
📞
The Call
Our AI voice agent calls each café directly during business hours. A natural conversation — just like you'd ask at the counter. "Hi, what's the price of a regular flat white?"
02
🧠
The Parse
Every response is transcribed and processed. We extract prices from natural speech — "$4.50", "four fifty", "about five bucks" — and flag ambiguous answers for human review.
03
📍
The Map
Verified prices are geocoded and published in real-time. Every data point links back to a real café, a real phone call, and a real price. No scraping. No guessing.
